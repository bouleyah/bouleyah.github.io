Okay, so what is this?

Boule (n) (Bool) - A round, crusty loaf of bread.
This is Bouleyah Breads , a small, home-based bakery by westsider Sean Trimpe, located in Harrison OH.

Nice! What's so special about Bouleyah Breads?

Bouleyah strives to perfect quality sourdough recipes that are 100% organic (no commercial yeast used). It started as a way to feed my family healthy breads that are low FODMAP and easy to digest. This led to expirmenting with unique flavors using homegrown organic produce, edible flowers, spices, and teas.

Whatever! How do I order?

Text 513.227.0282 with your order at least 3 days prior to one of my Bake Days. Reminders and cutoff times will be posted on my Facebook page. CHECK OUT this calendar for the next upcoming Bake Day and get your carbs on.
