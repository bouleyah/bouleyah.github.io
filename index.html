Ladies and Gents, Bouleyah, baby!!

Okay, so what is this?

This is Bouleyah Breads, a small, 100% organic, home-based bakery by a Westsider, Sean Trimpe. Look for a grand opening for pickups and delivery sometime Fall 2020.

Nice! What's so special about Bouleyah Breads?

Bouleyah strives to perfect quality sourdough recipes that are 100% organic. I started it as a way to feed my family healthy breads that are low fodmap and easy to digest. This led into expirmenting with unique flavors using homegrown organic produce, flowers, and spices.

What's the bread made with?

Organic ingredients is a simple way to put it. All of my breads are baked using the best quality, organically grown wheat I can get my hands on. The plan is to mill my own flours using local wheats soon!

I mean is it even possible to bake something to perfection?

No. But I love what I do and I love to fill my kitchen with the smell of freshly baked breads. By no means is my bread perfect, but each boule baked is a new leason learned. Baking breads at home has great rewards, but a lot can go wrong. What I give you is nothing short of the best that I can produce, from my home to yours.
